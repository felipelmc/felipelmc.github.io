My Work

What I have been doing lately

Data Scientist @ AtlasIntel

AtlasIntel provides advanced data products for political intelligence and economic forecasting. I'm currently working on calibrating survey samples, political analysis, public opinion monitoring, and improving internal data pipelines.

Researcher @ IESP-UERJ

MAPE · coordinated by Fernando Meireles & Bruno Schaefer

Policy and Election Monitoring and Evaluation Laboratory. Dedicated to studying public policy, elections, and their interconnections using causal inference and machine learning techniques.

DOXA · coordinated by Fernando Meireles & Argelina Cheibub Figueiredo

Laboratory of Electoral Studies, Political Communication and Public Opinion. A leading center for research on electoral processes, political communication, and voting behavior in Brazil.

NECON · coordinated by Fabiano Santos

Center for Studies on Congress. Focused on academic research on the Brazilian legislature, including databases on nominal votes, party migration, and legislative production.

Before joining AtlasIntel, MAPE, DOXA, and NECON, I provided data science and research consulting services to both industry (Roberto Marinho Foundation, Artplan, Visagio) and academia (FGV EBAPE, CEBRAP, GENI/UFF). Please check my CV for more information on my work experience.
